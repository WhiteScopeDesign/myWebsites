About us

Skimfast are an estabished Family owned company based in Tiptree, Essex with over 17 years experience in the trade. We cover all surrounding areas within a 30 mile radius.

We pride ourselves on having links to the most highly specialist staff to guarantee our quality workmanship and our attention to detail, all our staff are committed to providing you with the best possible service. All employees of SkimFast will strictly adhere to our Code of Conduct and Health and Safety Policy.

We understand, that any works to be carried out in your home are stressful. We do all we can to minimize hassle, mess and stress that it can cause.

We want to give you confidence in the whole process, so that you can concentrate on enjoying the end result.

We are British Gypson Approved Contractors

We are Wetherbys External Wall Insulation Approved Contractors and are qualified to NVQ Level 2 and NVQ Level 3 in specialist finishes and numerous City and Guilds.
